hive  joins are basicallyl of 3 types 
1) inner  joins 
2) Outer Joins 
3) map side joins 



1) inner Joins 
only matching row with given join condition wil result in output (intersection from both the table )

i)i.dno=r.dno;

2)Outer Joins .
matching & non matching both used to come in result depends on whta type of outer joins we are using .
Outer joins are further divided in to 3 part 

i) Left Outer Joins(matching from right and matching and non matching from left side table )
ii) Right Outer joins (Matching from left side table and Matching and non matching records from the right side table)
iii) Full outer joins (Matching & non-matching of left +non matching from right).
